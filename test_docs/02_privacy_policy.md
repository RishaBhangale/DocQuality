## 6. Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) Procedure

Individuals may exercise their data protection rights by submitting a request to privacy@dataflow-analytics.com or through our online DSAR portal at https://dataflow-analytics.com/privacy/dsar.

### DSAR Workflow:
1. **Submission**: Data subject submits request via portal or email
2. **Identity Verification**: Verified within 3 business days
3. **Acknowledgement**: Written acknowledgement within 5 business days
4. **Fulfillment**: Request completed within 30 calendar days (extendable by 60 days for complex requests)
5. **Documentation**: All DSARs logged in the DSAR Register

### Supported Rights:
- Right of access (Art. 15)
- Right to rectification (Art. 16)
- Right to erasure (Art. 17)
- Right to restriction (Art. 18)
- Right to data portability (Art. 20)
- Right to object (Art. 21)

## 7. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

DPIAs are conducted for any processing activity that presents high risk to data subjects, including:
- Large-scale processing of special categories of data
- Systematic monitoring of publicly accessible areas
- Automated decision-making with legal effects

## 8. International Data Transfers

All cross-border transfers are safeguarded by:
- EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where DPF is unavailable
- Transfer Impact Assessments conducted annually

## 9. Security Measures

- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- Annual penetration testing by independent third parties
- SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
