## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Information Security Management System (ISMS) policy establishes the framework for managing information security risks across TechCorp Global Ltd. The scope encompasses all information assets, personnel, processes, and technology infrastructure operated by TechCorp across all business units, including cloud-hosted services and third-party integrations.

The ISMS is aligned with **ISO/IEC 27001:2022** requirements and supports the organization's strategic objectives of maintaining client trust, regulatory compliance, and operational resilience.

## 2. Information Security Objectives

- Protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets
- Ensure compliance with applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations (GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2)
- Maintain business continuity through proactive risk management
- Foster a culture of security awareness across all organizational levels
- Achieve and maintain ISO 27001 certification

## 3. Risk Assessment Methodology

TechCorp employs a quantitative risk assessment methodology based on the ISO 27005 framework:

1. **Asset Identification**: All information assets are catalogued and classified
2. **Threat Assessment**: Threats are identified using STRIDE methodology
3. **Vulnerability Analysis**: Technical and procedural vulnerabilities are assessed quarterly
4. **Risk Evaluation**: Risks are scored using Likelihood × Impact matrices (5×5 scale)
5. **Risk Treatment**: Risks are treated through mitigation, transfer, avoidance, or acceptance

Risk assessments are conducted annually and upon significant changes to the threat landscape.

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)**: Overall accountability for the ISMS
- **Information Security Manager**: Day-to-day management and monitoring
- **Risk Owners**: Department heads responsible for risks in their domain
- **All Employees**: Compliance with security policies, incident reporting

## 6. Incident Management

All security incidents must be reported within 4 hours of detection through the SecureReport portal. The incident response team will triage, investigate, and remediate incidents following the documented Incident Response Plan (IRP-001).

## 7. Training and Awareness

Mandatory security awareness training is conducted annually for all employees. Role-based training is provided for personnel with elevated access privileges.

## 8. Continual Improvement

The ISMS is subject to annual management reviews and internal audits to ensure ongoing effectiveness and alignment with organizational objectives.
